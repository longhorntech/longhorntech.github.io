You@UT

We provide students at UT Austin mentorship and guidance to land internships at top companies in the tech industry. Incoming sophomores and juniors will receive a chance to learn and work with mentors that have previously navigated the recruiting process for many companies.

About

  • 1:1 Ratio
  • 2 Divisions

Our organization was founded by students who survived and eventually conquered the recruiting gauntlet, achieving offers from Google, Facebook, Uber, Quora, Robinhood, and many more. Regardless of which career path we chose, we all felt as though we couldn't have gotten where they did without the help of others -- help that we just happened to receive due to chance meetings, our own personal networks, and other similarly one-off efforts. We each recognized that we were lucky, and felt the need to expand access to the resources we used to achieve our career goals (coaching, mentorship, and teaching chief among them).

SWE@UT: Access to mentors that have received offers from all the top companies in the industry. 1-on-1 mentor-mentee pair up for direct guidance and support during the entire recruiting season. Access to an interview prep curriculum set up by LTA through various resources (CTCI, Leetcode) that ensures success in technical interviews. Bi-weekly (in the summer) and weekly (in the fall) mock interviews. Weekly lectures from mentors over common algorithms, data structures, and system architecture concepts.

Product@UT: Networking events for PM recruiters to inform/interact with UT students. Opportunities for prospective PM candidates to work with APMs/PMs to restructure their resume to focus on PM-relevant experience/attributes. Get exposure to PMs at different companies to get a better idea of what the companies are like + what PM is like at that company. Provide students 1-on-1 mentorship as they apply to companies, search for jobs, and want to learn more about PM in general.

Our Offers

Our grand vision will accommodate every form of working in the tech industry, including data science, design, and startup/high tech recruiting. For now, we will focus on our core competencies, derived from our founding members’ internship and career experiences: SWE recruiting at established tech firms, PM recruiting at established tech firms, and recruiting for large tech startups.
